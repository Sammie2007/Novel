The Court of the Empress of Death
​Located in the "Infinite Archive," the Court of Death is not a place of punishment, but a place of memory. The Empress herself is an ancient entity who keeps the records of every soul that has ever passed. She views the "Third Path" prophecy not as a religious event, but as a disruption to her records that must be corrected. Her heralds travel through the shadows, wearing silks dyed in the color of dried blood.
